The gameplay

When the player selects the "Story Mode", the game begins. We decided that the player would have a point of view in the first person, instead of seeing the player move in his environment, the player sees through his eyes, as if he was himself the character. This kind of view seems ideal for an Escape Game, which is meant to be the most immersive game possible for the player to fully engage in the story and challenges presented to him. It will begin in a short cinematic in which the character wakes up in a bed located inside a dark room. In the story we will implement an antagonistic character, it is because of this that the player is in the Escape Game. The antagonist goes to the beginning of the game to speak through a speaker to locate the basics of the story of DarkRoom. During the cinematics and the game phase, it will happen that the main character also speaks. These dialogs will be all in English, but with default French subtitles, which the player will be able to desactivate or modify in the options of the game. After the introductory cinematics, the player can begin to move his character in the room. At this point there will be a short tutorial in which the user can discover the keys that are useful to the game, he will learn how to move his character in one direction, how to pick up an object, how to observe a clue or open his inventory. Then the player will be free of these movements. The character is initially in a room of a building. His goal is to get out of the room to access the other two rooms on the floor, until he finishes the game by finding the last object on the floor. The player has a limited time of one hour, so it must spend a maximum of 20 minutes per piece. The three rooms he can access are:
• A room (1st room, where the character wakes up at the beginning of the game)
                                  • A bathroom / toilet (2nd room)
                                  • A laundry room (3rd and last room)
