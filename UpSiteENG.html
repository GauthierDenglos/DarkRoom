Updates in the website

The website du 23/04/2018 au 29/04/2018 -

After the defense 1 the website was only a base, with very little content, or not at all and just a few elements of oragnisation such as the header and the footer.

The goal of this week was to fill the site as much as possible, with both the elements of the defense 1, elements for future defenses, and aesthetic elements.

Globallement we have changed 3 aspects on this site: First colors, and aesthetics: Instead of having all white or all black, we decided to change the palette a little by adding different shades of gray, to better understand the site. A margin has been added to center everything and make reading more enjoyable, especially when texts are long. Added to this are the font changes, between titles, texts, and passages in italics (quote). The rest of the simple layout by playing with HTML.

Deuxièmement, l'organisation générale : Certaines parties ajoutées au premier abord n'étaient en fait pas necessaire, ou trop lourde pour être ajoutées au site, et donc réparties, ou supprimées. C'est le cas par exemple de tous les tableaux (aussi présents dans le cahier des charges), qui on été remplassé par cette partie Updates, remplie au fur et à mesure.

Thirdly, the filling: it is in doing this that the other points have come. By filling the site we realized the heaviness of some things, and the need to make the site more ergonomic. Despite this, filling is necessary, arrangements have been made and fill the site and now easier with the new organization. We know where this thing is, and what it should look like. This is the last step before having a complete site, which we can improve if we have the time.

- Gauthier Denglos @xcacaox
