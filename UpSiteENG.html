The website du 23/04/2018 au 29/04/2018 -

After the oral 1 the website was only a base, with very little content, or not at all and just a few elements of organisation such as the header and the footer.

The goal of this week was to fill the site as much as possible, with both the elements of the oral 1, elements for future orals, and esthetic elements.

Overall we have changed 3 aspects on this site:
First colors, and esthetic: Instead of having all white or all black, we decided to change the palette a little by adding different shades of gray, to better understand the site. A margin has been added to center everything and make reading more enjoyable, especially when texts are long. Added to this are the font changes, between titles, texts, and passages in italics (quote). The rest of the simple layout by playing with HTML.

Secondly, the general organization: Some parts added at first glance were actually not necessary, or too cumbersome to be added to the site, and thus distributed, or deleted. This is the case for example of all the tables (also present in the specifications), which have been replaced by this part "Updates", filled in as and when.

Thirdly, the filling: it is in doing this that the other points have come. By filling the site we realized the heaviness of some things, and the need to make the site more ergonomic. Despite this, filling is necessary, arrangements have been made and fill the site and now easier with the new organization. We know where this thing is, and what it should look like. This is the last step before having a complete site, which we can improve if we have the time.

- Gauthier Denglos @xcacaox
